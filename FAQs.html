Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of massages are on offer

I offer a variety of massage techniques tailored to meet your needs, including sports massage, swedish, deep tissue, aromatherapy, and more. Each massage is customised to address your specific concerns and preferences.

2. How long is a typical massage session?

Massage sessions typically range from 30 minutes to 90 minutes, allowing you to choose the duration that fits your schedule and wellness goals.

3. Do I need to make an appointment in advance?

Yes, I recommend scheduling your appointment in advance to ensure availability. You can easily book your session online through the website or by giving us a call.

4. What should I expect during my first massage session?

During your initial visit, you'll complete a brief intake form to discuss your health history and any specific areas of concern. We will then tailor the session to address your needs, ensuring a relaxing and rejuvenating experience.

5. Are you licensed and experienced?

Absolutely! I am fully licensed, highly trained with many years of experience in the field. I guarantee I have the expertise to provide you with exceptional care and results.

6. What should I wear during the massage?

You should undress to your level of comfort. Our therapists are trained to ensure your privacy and will use draping techniques to maintain your modesty throughout the session.

7. Are your massage oils and products safe for sensitive skin?

Yes, I use Detorra essential oils, these are high-quality massage oils and products that are safe for all skin types, including sensitive skin. If you have any specific allergies or concerns, please let me know, and I can accommodate accordingly.

8. What if I need to cancel or reschedule my appointment?

While I understand that emergency, unplanned issues and sickness can come up unexpectedly, please contact Gracie Massage via phone 089 216 9781 or email graciemassage@gmail.com or Instagram @Gracie_Massage AT LEAST 24 hours prior to your scheduled appointment date and time to avoid cancellation fees*.

*Cancellation is required 24 hours prior to appointment; failure to cancel within the required time will result in a fee of 50% of your appointment charge. A No Show is considered failure to cancel or failure to show for a scheduled appointment, the full amount will be required for the fail to cancel or no show appointment. Please give as much notice as possible to allow me the time to fill the appointment, and you will not be charged. This is a small business and my main source of income. Please be respectful of my cancellation policy.

No Shows and Late Cancellations (24 hours+)

Additionally, clients who schedule an appointment and simply DO NOT show up or cancel within the allotted timeframe of 24 hours will be required to pay a non-refundable deposit of half (50%) their scheduled session total in order to reschedule their next appointment.
