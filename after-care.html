After Care

After receiving bodywork like massage, it's important to be aware of the possibility of experiencing a healing crisis, also known as a Herxheimer reaction. This reaction occurs when the body attempts to eliminate toxins more rapidly than it can dispose of them, often resulting in symptoms such as fatigue, headaches, or emotional upheaval. However, it's crucial to interpret this reaction positively, recognizing it as a sign that the body is actively clearing out impurities and rebalancing itself. Healing encompasses not only physical detoxification but also emotional and spiritual cleansing, as the body has the remarkable ability to store memories and emotions within its tissues. Therefore, the release of these stored emotions during bodywork sessions offers an opportunity for holistic healing. To support the body's healing process, it's advisable to drink plenty of water, engage in gentle movement, prioritize rest and relaxation, consume nourishing foods, practice emotional self-care, and consider scheduling regular follow-up sessions with a qualified practitioner. These steps can optimize the benefits of bodywork, promote overall well-being, and support holistic healing on multiple levels.

Symptoms of healing crisis

  • Dizziness - lightheaded or fatigued.
  • Nauseous
  • Joint and muscle aches
  • Metallic or odd taste in the mouth
  • Intense emotional state
  • Frequent urination or defection, increase in sweating

Toxin Removal Support

  • Hydration: Supports toxin elimination by aiding the body's natural detoxification processes.
  • Fiber Intake: Supports swift removal of waste by promoting gastrointestinal system function.
  • Rest: Honoring feelings of tiredness or fatigue allows for essential recovery and rejuvenation.
  • Exercise: Promotes circulation, which assists in flushing out toxins through sweat and improved lymphatic drainage.
  • Nutrient-Rich Diet: Supports overall health and detoxification with essential vitamins and minerals.
