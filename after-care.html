After Care

When we receive body work (like a massage) it may result in what is known as healing crisis.

Healing crisis (herxheimer reaction) occurs when the body tries to eliminate toxins at a faster rate than the body can dispose of. The more toxins the more severe the healing crisis. It can be immediate or within several days after treatment. The reaction is a positive sign that the body is going through the process of clearing out impurities, toxins and imbalances.

Cleansing can be physical, emotional or spiritual, true healing involves all three. This is due to the body’s fascinating ability to store memories, emotions on the tissue, their recollection or release can accompany the manipulation of tissue. Suppressing emotions can lead to the emotions being stored in the body.

Symptoms of healing crisis

  • Dizziness - lightheaded or fatigued.
  • Nauseous
  • Joint and muscle aches
  • Metallic or odd taste in the mouth
  • Intense emotional state
  • Frequent urination or defection, increase in sweating

Ways to help support body with toxin removal and symptoms

  • Hydrate - will help aid the body in eliminating toxins
  • Increase Fiber - will support gastrointestinal system swift removal of waste
  • Sleep - feeling tired or fatigued needs to be honoured, rest to help with recovery
