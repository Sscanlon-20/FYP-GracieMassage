About Gracie Massage

Hi, I'm Sarah, and I'm a sports massage therapist here in Tramore. My journey into this field started with a deep-rooted passion for natural healing and a desire to help others feel their best. Back in 2017, I decided to make a career change and pursue my studies in Sports Massage Therapy. From the very beginning, I knew I wanted to work with sports teams and athletes. It's been an incredible journey since then. I've had the privilege of working with some of the leading jockeys here in Ireland, as well as with athletes from various disciplines like cycling, soccer, MMA, and running. Being on the sidelines as a first aid responder during games has given me invaluable insights into the specific needs of athletes and how I can best support them.

Over time, my approach to massage therapy has evolved to embrace a more holistic perspective. I've come to understand that true wellness encompasses not just the body, but the mind and spirit as well. This realisation has guided me to incorporate elements of relaxation and holistic healing into my practice, alongside traditional sports massage techniques. One of the most enriching aspects of my journey has been my exploration of essential oils. I've always been fascinated by their therapeutic properties, and I've spent years crafting my own blends using the highest quality oils. My fascination with essential oils led me to delve deeper into their potential for healing. That's what inspired me to undertake a course in aromatouch massage, where I learned to use a blend of eight different essential oils to create a truly immersive wellness experience for my clients. The combination of massage and aromatherapy creates a deeply relaxing and rejuvenating effect that promotes overall well-being.

Today, I'm proud to say that my therapy room has become a haven for those seeking relief from physical tension and a sanctuary for those in need of holistic healing. It's incredibly rewarding to see the positive impact that my work has on the lives of others, and I'm grateful every day to be able to do what I love.

Why I choose Sports massage?

I have always wanted to work with teams, soccer teams specifically, which led me on to work with an array of different sports athletes. I love the physical and psychological effects it has on the body and mind, how it can promote a sense of well-being, boost morale, relaxation and an escape.

I studied Level 3 Diploma In Sports Massage with IMST in Kilkenny, where we deep dived into the Anatomy and Physiology of the human body. I then furthered my studies by doing Level 4 Certified in Sports Massage. This was a dive into common sports injuries and how to identify and treat certain ailments with different techniques in sports massage. Since then, I've been upskilling through workshops doing Dry Cupping, Aromatouch Massage and first Aid. I've done lots of personal healing through inner child work, Kirtan meditation, yoga and sound healing. I would love to work in one of these areas down the line.
