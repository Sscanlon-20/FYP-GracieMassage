About Gracie Massage

Hi my name is Sarah, I work as a sports massage therapist, living in Tramore. I have always had a passion for natural healing and helping others feel good in their bodies. In 2017, I decided to make a career change and started studying Sports Massage Therapy.

I always wanted to work with teams, and I've had the pleasure of working with some leading top Jockeys here in Ireland. I've worked with an English Cycling team, a local soccer club, an MMA team in my local town, and numerous runners and cyclists. I've worked side pitch as a first aid responder. This has been an amazing experience. I’ve since taken on a more holistic approach to my massage techniques, promoting wellness through massage.

While studying, I developed a love for essential oils, and I started creating my own unique blends using high grade essential oils. These blends are available to purchase at my therapy room at Trá Beo Tramore. It was through my interest in essential oils that inspired me to do a course in aromatouch massage, using a blend of 8 different essential oils to create an overall wellness experience.

Why I choose Sports massage?

I have always wanted to work with teams, soccer teams specifically, which led me on to work with an array of different sports athletes. I love the physical and psychological effects it has on the body and mind, how it can promote a sense of well-being, boost morale, relaxation and an escape.

I studied Level 3 Diploma In Sports Massage with IMST in Kilkenny, where we deep dived into the Anatomy and Physiology of the human body. I then furthered my studies by doing Level 4 Certified in Sports Massage. This was a dive into common sports injuries and how to identify and treat certain ailments with different techniques in sports massage. Since then, I've been upskilling through workshops doing Dry Cupping, Aromatouch Massage and first Aid. I've done lots of personal healing through inner child work, Kirtan meditation, yoga and sound healing. I would love to work in one of these areas down the line.
