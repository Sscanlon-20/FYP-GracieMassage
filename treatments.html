Furthermore, the improved quality of sleep that often accompanies regular sports massage sessions contributes to overall recovery and rejuvenation. The release of toxins from the muscles not only aids in physical recovery but also enhances mental clarity and alertness. Overall, sports massage offers a holistic approach to well-being, addressing both the physical and psychological aspects of performance and health.
