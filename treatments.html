A sports massage is customised to meet the individual needs of clients, employing diverse techniques and movements aimed at optimizing performance and reducing the risk of injury or discomfort. Its advantages encompass enhanced flexibility and range of motion, alleviated muscle tension, improved overall well-being, reduced anxiety, better quality sleep, improved circulation, and the elimination of toxins from muscles.

Swedish Massage

Swedish massage is a whole-body therapeutic massage treatment that works the soft tissues and muscles to help restore health, creating a calming and balancing effect on the nervous system. Five main types of massage techniques are used during a Swedish massage: stroking and gliding, kneading, rubbing, tapping or pounding, and vibration. This therapy aids in muscle relaxation, enhances circulation, alleviates stress, relieves pain, and enhances flexibility.
